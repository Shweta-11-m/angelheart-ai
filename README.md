# 🧠 AngelHeart AI  
AngelHeart AI is a simple, AI-powered system designed to help people understand their emotions, reduce stress, and stay mentally balanced.
It combines mood tracking, AI support, journaling, and task management into one calm and connected experience.

## 🎯 Why this exists  

Most people don’t ignore their mental health because they don’t care.  
They ignore it because existing tools feel complicated, disconnected, or overwhelming.

You open one app for journaling.  
Another for tasks.  
Another for mental health.

But real life doesn’t work in parts.  
**Your emotions, thoughts, and actions are connected.**

---

## 💡 What AngelHeart AI does  

AngelHeart AI brings everything into one place.

It helps you:
- Track how you feel  
- Talk to an AI when you need support  
- Express thoughts easily (even by voice)  
- Manage tasks based on your mental state  

**Simple idea:**  
Understand your emotions → make better decisions → feel more in control  

---

## 📊 Impact  

- Makes emotional tracking quick and easy → improves consistency  
- Helps users notice stress patterns within a few days  
- Reduces mental overload by combining multiple tools into one  
- Provides instant support instead of delaying or suppressing emotions  

---

## ✨ Key Features  

### 🧭 Mood Dashboard  
Quickly log and understand your emotions → builds daily awareness without effort  

### 🤖 AI Chat Support  
Talk anytime → feel heard without judgment  

### 📊 Stress & Sleep Insights  
See patterns → understand what affects your mood  

### 🎙️ Voice Journaling  
Speak your thoughts → easier than typing when overwhelmed  

### ✅ Task & Habit Management  
Plan your day based on your mental state → avoid burnout  

---

## 🧠 How it works  

1. Check in with your mood  
2. AI understands your emotional state  
3. Get simple insights or suggestions  
4. Journal thoughts (text or voice)  
5. Manage tasks accordingly  
6. Track patterns over time
